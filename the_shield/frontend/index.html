Can I analyze only typed notes?

Yes. You can run live and full analysis using typed requirements without recording audio.

How are open questions tracked?

Each meeting keeps a running history of unresolved and resolved clarification questions.

Do I need to upload previous minutes?

No. Upload is optional and useful when continuing from earlier meetings.

Can I switch accounts safely?

Yes. Session controls let you login, logout, and switch account from the header.
